Kato's Pizzeria Vision Established in 2022

THE VISION

Be the most successful and fastest growing pizza chain in the world, recognized by consumers as the unmatched leader in value, quality, service and convenience.

MISSION

Kato's Pizzeria wants to be ""the best pizza chain by exceeding customer expectations with extraordinary value, great tasting products and outstanding people."" Founded in 2022, it was the result of a get together between Kato and the gang, who eventually got close and dropped their entire life savings into opening a pizza store just outside Jackson. It eventually became the fastest-growing pizza chain in the country.

WHY US

Little Kato's Pizzeria uses quality ingredients, including a blend of fresh mozzarella and cheddar cheese. They also use vine-ripened, fresh-packed Mississippi crushed tomatoes to make their sauce. At Kato's Pizzeria, one large topping tasty pizza goes for as low as $6
